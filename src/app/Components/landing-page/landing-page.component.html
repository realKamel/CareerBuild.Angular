Orange

Orange Egypt is one of Egypt’s leading telecommunications providers, offering a comprehensive range of services including mobile voice and data, fixed internet, and digital solutions. Established in 1998 as Mobinil, the company rebranded to Orange Egypt in March 2016 following its acquisition by the global telecom group Orange S.A.

Vodafon

is a leading telecommunications provider in Egypt, offering a wide range of services including mobile voice and data, fixed internet, and digital solutions. Established in 1998 as Click GSM, the company rebranded to Vodafone Egypt in 2002. As of November 2024, Vodafone Egypt serves approximately 45.9 million subscribers, making it the largest mobile operator in the country.

Realme

Realme entered the Egyptian market in 2019 and quickly gained popularity for offering reliable and stylish smartphones at budget-friendly prices. By July 2019, Realme had secured a 3.3% market share in Egypt, with 5% of all mobile sales in the EGP 2000–5000 segment. The brand was awarded “Fastest Growing Brand on E-Commerce Platforms” at the 2019 E-Commerce Summit in Egypt.
